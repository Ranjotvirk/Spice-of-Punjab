About Us

Spice of Punjab is a fully vegetarian restaurant dedicated to showcasing the richness, depth, and authenticity of North Indian cuisine.
Rooted in the vibrant culinary traditions of Punjab, we bring together time-honoured recipes, aromatic spices, and carefully selected ingredients to create an experience that is both refined and deeply satisfying.

Our philosophy is simple—vegetarian food should never compromise on flavour, quality, or presentation.
Every dish on our menu is thoughtfully crafted by experienced chefs who understand the balance of spice, texture, and aroma that defines true Punjabi cooking.
From slow-cooked curries and delicately spiced lentils to freshly baked breads straight from the tandoor, each plate reflects a commitment to authenticity and excellence.

At Spice of Punjab, we take pride in offering a diverse selection of vegetarian specialities that cater to both traditional tastes and modern preferences.
Whether you are enjoying rich, comforting classics or exploring bold street-style favourites, every bite is designed to deliver depth and character while remaining true to its origins.

Beyond the food, we aim to create an atmosphere that feels warm, welcoming, and elevated.
Our space is designed to complement the dining experience—perfect for family gatherings, friendly meals, celebrations, or quiet moments of indulgence.
We believe that great food is best enjoyed in a setting that reflects care, comfort, and hospitality.

Spice of Punjab is more than just a restaurant; it is a celebration of vegetarian cuisine at its finest.
We invite you to experience the essence of Punjab—authentic, elegant, and prepared with passion in every detail.
